Moment with Life Oyewunmi

Life Oyewunmi is a globally trained Hospitality and Etiquette Professional with a deep commitment to service excellence, leadership ethics, and personal growth. She is the founder of Moments with Life (MOL) a lifestyle project dedicated to inspiring excellence, character, and purpose-driven leadership. MOL has a division of Event Management, Ushering Services and Luxury Gifting Services.

Trained at esteemed institutions including Culinary Arts presented by The Corporate School of Etiquette (Savannah, Georgia, USA), Elocution, Etiquette and Ethics Academy (Nigeria), and Emma Dupont School of Etiquette (London). Life Oyewunmi also holds a B.Sc. in Accountancy from Redeemer's University, Nigeria. She is certified in CPR and First Aid by the American Heart Association and TAG Global Training, London. Life Oyewunmi reflects a well-rounded commitment to excellence and responsibility. She is known for her exceptional work in Green Room management, high-impact mentoring, and corporate training, equipping professionals with the skills and ethical grounding needed to thrive. She is a Fellow of the Chartered Institute of Mentoring and Coaching Nigeria (CIMCN).

She currently serves as Head of CSR for RCCG PSF and leads the Executive Care Unit. She is a proud member of Women in Hospitality, Nigeria. She is married to Oluwafemi Oyewunmi, a financial analyst, and they are blessed with a daughter.
